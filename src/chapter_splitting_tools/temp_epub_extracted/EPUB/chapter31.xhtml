Chapter 31

Afterword

Hello to those meeting me for the first time, and hello to those who haven't seen me in a while. I'm Aloha Zacho.

I am deeply grateful to everyone who picked up this book, to my editor, Mr. O, to Yuki-sama for providing wonderful illustrations for the work, and to everyone who has been reading my work online since before its publication.

The OSO series is currently being serialized in Dragon Age as a comic adaptation by Kurakumo Hane wa Jin-sensei. You can see the comical and cute comic version of Yun and the others' activities and adorable figures.

OSO will celebrate its 5th anniversary this time, and to commemorate it, a character popularity poll will be held on the special site of Fantasia Bunko.

I would be grateful if all the readers would vote for their favorite characters on the special site.

As in the previous volume, there are two works that I referred to when considering the isolated island area that became the stage for this volume.

One is "Uncharted 4," and I referred to the atmosphere of the pirate island that appears there and the gimmicks that appeared in it, and arranged them for OSO.

The other work is "Hunter x Hunter," and I created an item search to find the pirate king's treasure, being conscious of the specified cards in the Greed Island arc.

Just reading the explanations of the cards that appeared in the Greed Island arc, I, who was reading it during the "Weekly Shonen Jump" serialization, have very exciting memories.

Referring to that, I was writing while thinking that it would be fun to imagine the pirate king's personality from the 108 items, each with a word from the pirate king added to it.

What kind of pirate king was born in your minds?

Perhaps you can see an unexpected side from the explanations of other treasures.

I hope you will continue to support me, Aloha Zacho.

Finally, I would like to express my gratitude once again to all the readers who picked up this book.

February 2019 Aloha Zacho
