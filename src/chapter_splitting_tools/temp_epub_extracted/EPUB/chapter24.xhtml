"Emefand Turtle. Yun, you're lucky."

And, in proportion to the high stats, the experience points gained from combat are also delicious.

"I can't, I can't attack. It's so charming and cute that I want to feed it rather than defeat it and stroke its shell."

The Emefand Turtle's vacant expression and round eyes are so endearing, and I feel a sense of pity for its very slow movements.

In simple terms, it can be said to be the way summoning beasts are used in a certain famous RPG.
