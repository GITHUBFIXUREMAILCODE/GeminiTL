"──Kuaaaaaaooooooooon!"

It lifted its relaxed tentacles again and swung them down as if slamming them onto the beach.

"Everyone, evade!"

The shadow of the tentacle lifted overhead fell on the beach, and the vanguard fled left and right to avoid it.

And the moment the tentacle slammed onto the beach, fine sand swirled up, and the ground shook, hindering the movement of the rearguard who were outside the range of the tentacle.

"Everyone, the next attack is coming!"

Mew, who had escaped into the air by leaping with her [Three-Dimensional Restriction Release] sense, attacked a nearby tentacle without being affected by the ground shaking.

But the Kraken, targeting the vanguard who had stopped due to the ground shaking, attempted to grab them with its tentacles.

"Seriously, me again!"

Gantz, who had been thrown into the sea by a tentacle grab attack during a previous encounter with the Kraken, shouted and was grabbed by the tentacle and thrown.

"──《Aqua Wall》!"

"──《Mud Pool》!"

"Normally, with this intense an attack, its HP would be shaved off at a pretty good rate, but... ──《Ice Lance》simultaneous volley!"

Sei-nee created over thirty ice lances in the air and launched them at the Kraken, but more than half of them were blocked by the tentacles the Kraken raised, reducing the damage.

In addition, the attacks of the magicians Kohaku, Rirei, Mami-san, and the two catapults of the [OSO Fishing Cooperative] were also being received by opening the tips of the tentacles and crushing them.

"It can't be helped. The Kraken's resistance changes depending on the part that's hit."

The Kraken as a raid boss that appears in the inlet of this remote island can now be defeated with the appearance of an HP bar, and its basic behavior pattern is not much different from when we encountered it in the event battle.

However, as a characteristic of the Kraken that was discovered after it became defeatable, it has damage reduction abilities for each part.

The Kraken can be divided into three parts: the triangular, slender body, the head with eyes and mouth, and the leg part including the 10 tentacles.

The damage inflicted on the Kraken is highest on the head, and attacking the vitals between the eyebrows stops its movement for 30 seconds.

Next, the largest and most conspicuous body part has about 30% damage reduction.

Finally, the tentacles, which obstruct various attacks, inflict more damage on the Kraken the closer they are to the base, but the tips of the tentacles that defend against the rearguard's attacks reduce damage by about 90%.

"──《Powerful Bow Technique - Mountain Breaker》! Damn it, I've been bewitched!"

I also unleashed an art to aim for the weak point marker of 《Foodstuff Expertise》 that had reappeared on its head over time.

However, the trajectory was blocked by the Kraken's raised tentacle, slightly shifting my aim just before firing, and the arrow pierced through the edge of the Kraken's body as if gouging it out.

"Don't underestimate us! ORAAAAA!"

Shichifuku, who had dashed out, leaped onto the tentacle and used it as a foothold to thrust a harpoon into the Kraken's brow.

"Alright, I've made it immobile for another 30 seconds!"

Shichifuku succeeded in attacking the vital point between the eyebrows, which I had failed to do earlier, and in response, attacks from allies were carried out again.

The rearguard, while adding attacks sporadically until now, had all prepared their ultimate moves for this moment.

"──《Ice Lance》! Volley!"

Sei-nee fired more than thirty ice spears, more than before due to the [Delay] skill, at the Kraken's body.

Shichifuku was running across the sandy beach, pulling out the harpoon he had thrust in to escape from the aftermath of the magic.

"Fufufu! Let's quickly defeat it and continue our fun at the beach with the beautiful girls. ──《Prominence Dragoon》!"

"《Spatial Enchant》──Attack, Defense, Speed!"

While paying attention to the Kraken's pre-motions, I pinpointed and shot at places with good damage penetration, such as the head and body, and raised a warning about the Kraken's pre-motions.

The Kraken began to raise the tentacles it had been swinging down on the beach high, and I issued guidance instructions based on that movement.

While the vanguard and rearguard alike escaped to the left side, which divided the crescent-shaped beach into three parts, the Kraken's tentacles slammed into the sea surface, causing a tsunami.
