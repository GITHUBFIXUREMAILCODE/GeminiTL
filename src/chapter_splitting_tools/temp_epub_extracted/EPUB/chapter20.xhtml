Chapter 20

It has an effect similar to the 【Land Emperor Turtle Medal】 that I obtained when I cleared the quest in the Grand Rock's body dungeon, a Super Dreadnought-class MOB.

It's a faintly translucent bluish medal, but its hue subtly changes depending on the angle of the sunlight, making it beautiful.

I participated in the kraken subjugation for this item, so I'm happy to have obtained it.

And the other item is a drop item from the subjugation.

"Um, the items I got are the 【Sea Emperor Squid Medal】 and... the 【Kraken's Outer Mantle】?"

"Oh, that's a good item for you, Yun-oneechan!"

The kraken's outer mantle, taken out from the inventory, is a material with a smooth, reddish-brown, vinyl-like texture.

I checked the item's stats and understood that it was indeed useful to me.

"Oh, it's a reinforcement material for armor and accessories."

"In Yun-chan's case, your armor has a 【Recognition Inhibition】 reinforcement effect, so I think you can use it to strengthen that."

The Ocher Creator armor and the 【Phantom Dweller】 mantle, which I usually use as accessories, have 【Recognition Obfuscation】, which is a higher level than 【Recognition Inhibition】, so it seems I can use it for further strengthening.

"This is kind of nice. Should I ask Claude to incorporate it next time? So, Miu and Sei-nee, what did you get?"

"I got kraken bones. It's a production material, but I don't know how to use it, so I'm going to take it to Magi-san next time."

Miu said, taking out a white, cloudy, thin, wide bone.

"I got kraken bones too. I really wanted a rare reinforcement material, though..."

I smiled wryly at Sei-nee, who drooped her shoulders, wondering if her usual material-desire sensor hadn't worked and she didn't get it.

"Hey, Sei! Miu-chan, Yun-jouchan, why don't we take a break and play here again?"

Mikazuchi waved his hand from under the beach parasol, calling us over.

"Sei-oneechan, don't be down, let's log out once and then play here again!"

"Okay, that's right."

We logged out together in a sleep state near the parasol where Mikazuchi and the others were.

In the real world, I had lunch with Mihane, and Miu logged back in a step ahead of me, and I also logged in, delayed by clearing the dishes.

"Ugh, ah, un~!"

I stretched out my stiff body when I logged in, as I had logged out in a seated position under the beach parasol with my knees drawn up.

I looked around with eyes still waking up from the sleep state, and a large shadow was spread around the parasol.

When I moved my gaze to follow the source of the shadow, a gigantic structure was standing there that hadn't been there before logging out.

"...What is this?"

The structure, with a stone base and an undulating ice slide, was both mystical and somehow familiar.

"Ah, Yun-oneechan, you're finally awake!"

Miu, holding a large float, found me and called out from the sea.

"How is it! A giant slider made with Sei-oneechan's ice magic and Mami-san's earth magic! The catapult was broken and we can't dive, so we had them make it!"

Miu said, proudly explaining that Sei-nee and Mami-san, who had finished their break and logged back in earlier, had made it, and took my hand.

"Let's slide down the giant slider together! It'll be fun!"

『『──Kyaaaaaaah!』』

Immediately after that, I heard the voices of Minuit and Mami-san screaming as they slid down the slider with tremendous force behind Miu.

One scream sounded like they were having fun, the other was a scream of fear and tension, and I stiffened my body.

"O-oh, I'm fine. You go have fun by yourself, Miu."

"Ehh, it's no fun alone~. Besides, the slider's momentum is too strong, and if you ride the float alone, your weight is too light and you get thrown out at the curves. Look──"

『Aaaaaaah!』

As Miu pointed, Gantz, who couldn't make it around the curve of the slider, was thrown out of the float at the curve and slammed straight into the surface of the sea.

When diving from the catapult, he had been diving into the sea with a narrow area of his body to reduce the impact of landing, but this time, he was landing with the front of his body.

As a result, Gantz was floating on the surface of the inlet with a large amount of terrain damage and the 【Stun】 abnormal status.

I was dumbfounded by the sight, but I quickly snapped back to my senses and admonished Miu.

"Miu, that slider is dangerous! It's dangerous if you fall!"

"It's okay, it's okay! That kind of thing only happens rarely!"

"I don't wanna!"

I resisted, but Miu, who had high ATK stats, overpowered me and dragged me into the ice structure.

Entering the structure while receiving lukewarm gazes from the surroundings, the base was made of stone walls and covered with ice, so it was dim and cold.

For that reason, Miu lit the way with 《Light》 magic and went up the stone steps, reaching the mouth of the slider about 10 meters high.

"Now, Yun-oneechan, let's go!"

Miu placed a large float and urged me to sit on the back half, with her riding on the front half.

"Damn..."

I sat in the back, trying to grab the edge of the float, but Miu stopped me.

"You'll fall off like that, here, get close to me like this!"

"A-ah!"

Miu took my hand, and I wrapped my arms around her from behind, around her stomach.

I'm often hugged by Miu, but hugging her like this makes me a little embarrassed.

"Okay, let's go! Go!"

"Whoa, uwaah!?"

We slid down the steep ice slider with the float all at once to accelerate at the beginning.

I reflexively put strength into the arms I had around Miu, and closed my eyes to endure the swaying movement of the slider, but gradually got used to it and cautiously opened my eyes.

"Ah, beautiful..."

As we slid down with great momentum, water droplets and ice particles were stirred up, reflecting and rushing backward in the air.

I was impressed by the sight of looking at the inlet from a high position.

"Yun-oneechan, how is it? Is it fun?"

"Yeah, it's fun. Thank you."

When I answered, tilting my head back to look at Miu, she looked happy with an ehehe.

But what was spreading out ahead of the biggest curve for us was──

"Hey, Miu! Look ahead, ahead! There's no continuation of the slider there!"

"Oh, I guess it melted and fell off because it's ice?"

"This is no time to be so leisurely, kaaaaaah!"

We, riding on the float sliding down the slider with great momentum, were thrown into the air as is.

In a moment of weightlessness, I hugged Miu from behind so as not to let her go.

"Kyaaaaaaah!"

While Miu happily screamed in free fall, I activated the telekinetic skill on myself and Miu.

"Uwaaah! Yun-oneechan, you're flying, you're flying!"

We, who had our weight reduced by the telekinetic skill, glided with the momentum of jumping out of the slider.

Miu was excited about gliding, but I, who was maintaining 《Kinesis》, had no room to return a word.

Then, we landed on the surface of the sea from the float, splashing a lot of water, and finally breathed a sigh of relief.

"...Yun-oneechan, that was fun. One more time!"

"Absolutely not!"

I refused resolutely, but she turned sparkling eyes towards me.

This time, I was able to subjugate the raid boss kraken smoothly without any particular worries, but in the end, I had a bad experience for my heart in an unexpected place, and I sighed alone, utterly.
